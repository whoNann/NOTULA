## Brand & Style

The design system is engineered for deep focus and cognitive clarity. It targets knowledge workers, researchers, and developers who require a friction-less environment for capturing thought. The aesthetic is **Modern Minimalist** with a technical edge, characterized by a "dark-mode-first" philosophy that reduces eye strain during long writing sessions. 

The personality is professional, intelligent, and unobtrusive. While the interface remains grounded and utilitarian, subtle **Glassmorphism** and soft glowing accents are used exclusively for AI-driven features to signal a layer of "intelligence" beneath the surface. The UI should feel like a high-end tool—precise, responsive, and quiet.
